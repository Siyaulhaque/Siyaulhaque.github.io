About Me

I’m Siya ul Haque, a naturalist and explorer with countless stories from my time in the wild. While I don’t consider myself a writer, this space is my effort to document and share my experiences—observations from nature, encounters with wildlife, and the places I’ve traveled.

Beyond exploration, this blog also serves as a journal—a place to capture memorable moments with people, sharing photos and reflections like a diary. Every interaction, every journey, and every story holds meaning, and this is where I keep those memories alive.

This is a collection of nature, life, and the wonders that inspire me.
