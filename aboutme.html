Siya Ul Haque

I’m Siya Ul Haque, a naturalist and explorer with countless stories from my time in the wild. While I don’t consider myself a writer, this space is my effort to document and share my experiences. Observations from nature, encounters with wildlife, and the places I’ve traveled.

Beyond exploration, this blog also serves as a journal. A place to capture memorable moments with guests, sharing photos and reflections like a diary. Every interaction, every journey, and every story holds meaning, and this is where I keep those memories alive.

My Journey

I have been fascinated by the natural world since childhood. From hiking through forests to studying ecosystems, my goal is to bring people closer to nature through my research and documentation.
